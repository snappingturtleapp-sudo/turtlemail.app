Corporate Privacy Architecture

We operate a strict, registration-free checkout workspace to maximize global data compliance. This document outlines how recipient parameters flow through our processing infrastructure.

What We Collect

When an enterprise partner initiates a bulk campaign or a manual domestic checkout, we capture the final recipient names and physical delivery addresses exclusively to facilitate shipping and logistics.

Why We Collect It

This information is processed solely to fulfill physical product deliveries via external printing lanes and to ensure your organization complies with standard legal tax and regional accounting obligations.

Third-Party Sharing

Delivery details are passed securely over memory layers directly to our global print-on-demand and fulfillment partner, Prodigi. Prodigi processes this data strictly as a downstream data processor on our behalf.

Data Retention Limits

To eliminate systemic liability, we explicitly opt out of saving local email records or persistent database rows containing recipient data. Financial totals and anonymous batch order numbers are kept for the minimum duration required by tax authorities, while raw address arrays vanish from system RAM post-submission.
